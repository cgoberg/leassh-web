**Recommended Hero:**

**Recommended "What makes Leassh different":**
1. **SSH Architecture** - "The only parental control that works through SSH — no apps to install on your child's devices, no software they can uninstall or tamper with"

2. **True Cross-Platform** - "Monitor Android phones, Chromebooks, and Linux machines that other tools can't touch. One solution for your entire mixed-device household."

3. **Local Privacy Option** - "Keep your family's data on your home network — never send activity logs to the cloud. The only parental control with true data sovereignty."

4. **Process-Level Understanding** - "See exactly what's running: Python scripts, Blender projects, game mods. Not just 'screen time' but actual digital creativity."
